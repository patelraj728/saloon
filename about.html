The Genesis.

Founded in 2018, ELIXIR emerged from a desire to strip back the noise of the traditional salon experience. We envisioned a space that felt less like a commercial enterprise and more like a private, curated atelier.

Every detail, from the ambient acoustics to the tactility of our styling chairs, has been rigorously considered. It is an exercise in restraint—where what is omitted is just as important as what remains.

E. Laurent Founder & Creative Director
